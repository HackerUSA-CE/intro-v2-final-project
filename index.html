About Us

A man walked into a bar with his alligator and asked the bartender, "Do you serve lawyers here?" "Sure do," replied the bartender. "Good," said the man. "Give me a beer and I'll have a lawyer for my gator."

For the last 3 years, Juris Foodence has served lawyers as well as judges, plaintiffs, defendants, and anyone else who enjoys great food with an even greater pun. We have expanded from our location near the courthouse to a new moving violation - our food truck!

Juris Foodence is owned by Jack Hoki and was inspired by his father Bob, a retired judge who ruled with wisdom on the bench and a wacky sense of humor everywhere else.
